About Us


Feature Planet Private Limited is a ISO 9001:2015 Certified company incorporated on 18 September 2017. It is classified as Non-govt Company and is registered at Registrar of Companies, Bangalore.

Feature Planet Private Limited is a manpower providing service-based company. With its roots in handling multiple services for the Government and private companies. We at our company provide the robust services and have also, helped our employees reach holistic learning. We are committed in providing the best manpower services.

Our expertise of work has been in various Information collecting fields, the ongoing various projects that we have taken up were very much helpful in collecting the required data and simplifying the work of the Government in giving the beneficiaries fast services.

The company mainly strives to decrease the burden of manual work and reduce the human error that often leads to delay in the process. Over the years many of our employees have been trained to handle various types of jobs which require IT expertise.

Our clients have been Government departments and private companies and have completely believed and trusted in our work and also the privacy concern that we strictly adhere in our policy.
